Mexican-Street-Corn

Ingredients

  • 4 corn ears, shucked
  • 4 corn ears, shucked
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • ½ cup Cotija cheese, grated
  • chili powder
  • cilantro, chopped fresh

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to a medium-high heat.
  2. Mix mayo, lime juice and cumin while grill is heating up. Set aside.
  3. Grill your corn on the cob for 7-9 minutes or until hot and slightly charred.
  4. As soon as you're done grilling, roll the cob in melted salted butter and then in the mayo mix.
  5. Sprinkle with Cotija cheese, chili powder and chopped cilantro and serve immediately.
